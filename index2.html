Josef Hruza

The most amazing slayer of elves

Fought bravely under the Lonely mountain. Killed countless elves, held the line against overwhelming odds. He never took a step back and the poor enemy, unable to advance, had to summon archers to snipe Josef, before any progress could be made. Even then, they did not succeed.

He died in the chaos of a rout when our lines broke on the southern slopes far away from where he stood. His bravery could not stop that disaster, and so he paid with his life for the cowardice of others.
